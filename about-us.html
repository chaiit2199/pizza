We Are Not Just
A Restaurant,
We Are A Family!!

Pizza Shop Since 1986.

Basilico Pizza was born out of three friends’ shared passion for simple, quality pizza.

We’ve been awarded Best Value Eats in the Observer Food Monthly awards in 2022, after winning their Best Cheap Eats award in 2017, have been voted London’s favourite pizza in Time Out’s inaugural Clash of the Slices in 2022, and were named London’s most-loved restaurant in Time Out’s 2016 Love London Awards.

Bringing a dining table to life with great food, great music and great company. Lifting glasses and spirits.

All those PizzaExpress favourites you know and love have crossed countries and continents.
